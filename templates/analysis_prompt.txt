- Low activity may indicate abandonment OR project maturity.
- High churn may indicate instability OR active refactoring.

Additional interpretation rules:

- When interpreting metrics such as developer_turnover, commit volatility, or activity cadence, always mention at least one alternative interpretation.
- Do not assume that high developer_turnover automatically indicates instability.
- Consider that OSS projects often have many occasional contributors.
- Explicitly mention uncertainty when multiple interpretations exist.
